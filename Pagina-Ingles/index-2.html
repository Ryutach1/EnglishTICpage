Operating Systems Review

Windows 11

Pros: More software compatibility than MacOS, the most user-friendly for beginners.

Cons: Less secure than Linux, updates are slower than MacOS.

MacOS Ventura

Pros: Most optimized for Apple hardware, smoother than Windows for creative apps.

Cons: Less customizable than Linux, more expensive than Windows.

Linux Ubuntu

Pros: The most secure and lightweight, free unlike Windows/MacOS.

Cons: Harder to use than Windows, fewer supported apps.

Advice Corner

For Students

Recommendation: A Windows laptop (e.g., Dell XPS) because it’s more affordable than MacBooks and has more educational software.

For Business Professionals

Recommendation: A MacBook Pro—it’s the most reliable for productivity and has better battery life than most Windows laptops.

For Gamers

Recommendation: An Asus ROG with Windows—it’s the most powerful and has more game compatibility than MacOS/Linux.
